About Our Practice

We're The Dental Experts You Can Trust

Established since the 1920s, 372 Dental is a well-regarded dental practice situated on Bury Road in Bolton. We pride ourselves on providing a professional and caring service at all times, whilst maintaining the highest quality of work.

Our practice comprises three newly renovated surgeries, fully equipped with the latest technology used in modern dentistry. All our records are fully computerised, and our surgeries are situated on the ground floor with easy access for wheelchair users.

Under the ownership of Stephen Bloor and Ross Mathieson, we have created a peaceful environment that is fresh, modern and welcoming. Our practice ethos is to promote dental health at all times, and we recommend regular check-ups at intervals agreed between you and your dentist in Bolton.

From your first assessment, we aim to ensure that you receive the highest levels of care available in preventive and precision dentistry. We will take the time to listen to your concerns and discuss all available treatment options with you.

New Patients

New Patients Welcome

First-time adult patients will fall under our Private Care Plan (pay as you go) or the Denplan Scheme (monthly budget payment). Denplan allows you to spread the cost of your routine dental care evenly throughout the year, so there are no surprise bills.

Children and patients under 18 years of age will fall under the NHS Registration Scheme, which is free of charge. We are proud to be an NHS dentist in Bolton that continues to welcome young patients.

To register as a new patient or to find out more about our options, please contact our reception team on 01204 522 581 or visit our new patients page.

Emergency Care

Need Emergency Dental Care?

If you need emergency dental assistance and our dentists are busy at that moment, please do not worry. Call us immediately on 01204 522 581 and we will ensure you are given an appointment on a priority basis.

As a registered private or Denplan patient, you can also access weekend emergency cover between 9am and 5pm on Saturdays. For full details on what to do in a dental emergency in Bolton, please visit our dedicated emergency page.
