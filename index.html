New Patients

New Patients Welcome

First-time adult patients will fall under our Private Care Plan (pay as you go) or the Denplan Scheme (monthly budget payment). Denplan allows you to spread the cost of your routine dental care evenly throughout the year, so there are no surprise bills.

Children and patients under 18 years of age will fall under the NHS Registration Scheme, which is free of charge. We are proud to be an NHS dentist in Bolton that continues to welcome young patients.

To register as a new patient or to find out more about our options, please contact our reception team on 01204 522 581 or visit our new patients page.

Emergency Care

Need Emergency Dental Care?

If you need emergency dental assistance and our dentists are busy at that moment, please do not worry. Call us immediately on 01204 522 581 and we will ensure you are given an appointment on a priority basis.

As a registered private or Denplan patient, you can also access weekend emergency cover between 9am and 5pm on Saturdays. For full details on what to do in a dental emergency in Bolton, please visit our dedicated emergency page.

We treat all common dental emergencies including severe toothache, broken or chipped teeth, lost fillings or crowns, dental abscesses, and knocked-out teeth.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you accepting new NHS patients?

Yes. Children under 18 can register under the NHS Registration Scheme free of charge. Adults are welcome to register under our Denplan monthly payment plan or as private pay-as-you-go patients. Please contact reception on 01204 522 581 for current availability.

Do you offer same-day emergency appointments?

Yes. If you are experiencing dental pain, a broken tooth, or any other dental emergency, please call us immediately. We will do our best to see you on the same day.

What is Denplan and how does it work?

Denplan is a monthly payment plan that covers your routine dental care, including check-ups and hygienist visits. It spreads the cost evenly so there are no unexpected bills. Contact us for a personalised quote based on your oral health.

Where is 372 Dental located?

We are located at 372 Bury Road, Bolton, BL2 6DD. All our surgeries are on the ground floor with easy access for wheelchair users. There is on-street parking available nearby. Get directions →

How do I book an appointment?

You can book by calling us on 01204 522 581 during our opening hours, or by visiting our new patients page to request an appointment online.
